Getting There

We suggest booking a cheap flight to a city in Europe and then booking a flight to Portugal from there. Getting to Europe is the most challenging, but travel within Europe is really easy and inexpensive! Exitfares (https://www.exitfares.com) is a great place to watch for deals to Europe from San Francisco, Minneapolis, Chicago, Washington D.C., or Los Angeles. Momondo (http://www.momondo.com/) has a lot of intereuropean flight options in a pretty interface!

Some people mentioned using the stop before to see a new city and others are just stopping for a layover. Places we’re excited about that would be great for a short visit are Madrid, Spain; Budapest, Hungary, and Stockholm, Sweden.

Lisbon

The airport you’ll fly into is in Lisbon, Portugal. (LIS) Sintra is approximately 30km from Lisbon, easily accessible by train or car. We suggest you stay in Sintra for the night before and night of the wedding.
